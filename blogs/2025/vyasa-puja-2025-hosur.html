Vyasa Puja at Guhai Kovil 2025

Published on July 10, 2025

Event: Vyasa Puja at Guhai Kovil 2025

Location: Hosur

Date: 10th July 2025

Time: 8:00 AM Morning

Guhai Swamiji performed special pujas to Ganapathi, Kali Devi and Sri Sadashiva Brahmendral on the auspicious occasion of Vyasa Puja / Gurupoornima Day. Gho Puja was also performed.

Vyasa Puja, also known as Guru Purnima, is a sacred day dedicated to honoring and expressing gratitude to spiritual teachers. This celebration commemorates the birth anniversary of Sage Vyasa, who compiled the Vedas and wrote the Puranas and the Mahabharata.

The day began with traditional rituals and offerings to honor the divine presence. Devotees gathered to receive blessings from Guhai Swamiji and participate in this spiritually significant event.

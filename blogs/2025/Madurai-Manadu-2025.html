Muruga Manadu Madurai 2025

July 1, 2025

Event: Muruga Manadu Madurai 2025

Location: Madurai

Date: 1st July 2025

Swamiji graced the spiritual congregation as a chief guest, organized by RSS & Vishwa Hindu Parishad More than 5 Lakh people attended the function and recited Kanda Shashti Kavacham together. Key political leaders Sri Annamalai (Tamil Nadu BJP Leader) & Sri Pavan Kalyan(Deputy CM of Andhra Pradesh) also attended the function.
